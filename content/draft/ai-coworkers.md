# AI co-workers

<p class="blog-post-date">February 9, 2026</p>

I struggle to articulate the depth of ignorance, vacuousness, arrogance, contempt and devaluation of human beings that is imbued in statements like "AI agents are your new co-workers".

I mean the utter gall, the incredible disrespect of all the wonderful people I've had the pleasure and honor of working with in my career.

I don't understand why we let the people with the saddest, most impoverished and shrivelled-up view of existence tell us what the world is like and how it's is going to be.

I don't know why they're here. But it's not why I'm here.

Yes, it cuts both ways. When people make the insane declaration that these mindless chatbots are on par with humans, they are also declaring that there is nothing more to humans than to these mindless chatbots.

I mean we've also ascribed agency to trees and rocks and invented ghosts so... I think it's comical that we think that we'd be any good at the Turing game.

"Humans have lost faith in ourselves."

Diminishing ourselves. Climbing into the Procrustean bed.
