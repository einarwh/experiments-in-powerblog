# Superpowers

<p class="blog-post-date">February 8, 2025</p>

I don't know who needs to hear this, but it's some of you. Humans don't have superpowers. This is by definition: what makes the power super is that it's beyond human capabilities. We are not superheroes, we are human.

Yes, yes, I know, you'll say I'm being too serious, I should take my Pepcid and smile more.

it's not serious in a sense, it's just a bit of harmless and whimsical fun

But it's not harmless. Calling it superpowers mystifies it, and it's not mysterious. It makes it sound like something innate. Abstracts away what it is and mystifies it.
